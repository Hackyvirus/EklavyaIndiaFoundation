Welcome to Eklavya India Foundation

Eklavya India Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) with a steadfast commitment to nurturing the dreams and aspirations of thousands of students. Established five years ago, our mission is to empower students by providing them with opportunities to achieve academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Vision

At Eklavya India Foundation, we envision a world where every student has the chance to access quality education, irrespective of their socio-economic background. We believe that education is the key to unlocking one's potential and creating a brighter future. Meet Our Founder and CEO


We are working from last 5 years

Mr. Raju Kendre, our visionary Founder and CEO, is the driving force behind our organization. His unwavering dedication to education and youth empowerment has paved the way for countless success stories.
